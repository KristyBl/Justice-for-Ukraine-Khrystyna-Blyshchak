Self-Care Awareness

Your well-being is of the utmost importance. If you find the content on this page too distressing or emotionally overwhelming, it is strongly encouraged that you leave the page and seek support or assistance from friends, family, or professionals. Remember, it is okay to step away if you feel unwell while reading the material.

War crimes committed by Russia include, but are not limited to...
